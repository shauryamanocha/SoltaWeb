  • Solta is an initiative focusing on the mystery of cosmic rays
  • We have been working on several different demonstrations to illustrate the wonders of cosmic rays
  • One of which is an app, using a regular phone camera to see cosmic rays

Our App

  • The app also tracks user statistics using Google's backend system, Firebase
  • This allows users to see information on a global scale
  • The mapping ability shows 'hotspots' for more cosmic ray detections

  • Our app captures the mysteries of cosmic rays without any special materials
  • The app is currently in development for both iOS and Android
  • With this app you can see the wonders of physics right before your eyes on your smartphone

Our Mission

  • With this initiative we hope to help spread the world about one of physics' greatest mysteries, cosmic rays.
  • We also hope to inform and inspire youth about physics and cosmic rays by providing demonstrations
